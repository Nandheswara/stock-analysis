## Understanding Good vs Bad Values

### Good Indicators:
- ✅ Liquidity: "Good" or "Excellent"
- ✅ Quick Ratio: > 1.0
- ✅ Debt/Equity: < 1.0 (except banks)
- ✅ ROE: > 15%
- ✅ ROA: > 5%
- ✅ High Promoter Holdings (>50%)

### Warning Signs:
- ⚠️ High Debt/Equity (>2.0)
- ⚠️ Low Quick Ratio (<0.5)
- ⚠️ Negative EBITDA growth
- ⚠️ Very high Beta (>1.5)
