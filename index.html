Quick Start
  1. Click on "Start Analysis" above or "Fundamental Analysis" in the menu
  2. Enter an Indian stock symbol (e.g., RELIANCE, TCS, INFY)
  3. Click "Add Stock" to add it to your analysis
  4. Click "Edit" to enter fundamental data manually
  5. Add more stocks to compare them side by side
  6. Remove stocks you don't need with the "Delete" button
